Portal Services have three categories:

T&M - Typically fixed price projects for a non-revenue or hybrid service  (e.g. Vital Records, CMLS, Marriage System, Web Sites)

Hosting - We provide hosting for non-revenue services (e.g. website hosting, third party software, database replication)

Transactional / Self-Funded - Services that have a fee/payment associated that either the filer/fee-payer or the department pays to HIC.
