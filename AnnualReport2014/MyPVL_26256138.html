HIC - Annual Reports : MyPVL

Created by Rosie Warfield, last modified by Jing Xu on Nov 12, 2014

MyPVL (https://pvl.ehawaii.gov/mypvl) is the new home for all professional and vocational licensees.  It provides a central dashboard for all licensees to access their license information and services.  Previously a single licensee needs to access three or more website to conduct business with the Department of Commerce and Consumer Affairs, Profession and Vocational Licensing (PVL) Branch, utilizing PVL Search to view their licensee information, i.e. status, expiration date, etc, PVL Renew to complete the renewal and payment, Real Estate Continuing Education System to check on their continuing education (CE) requirements and status.  MyPVL brought all three services into a single location.

The service was created in corporation with PVL under theMy.Hawaii.gov initiative.  By linking the portal eHawaii.gov account with the PVL licenses, the licensee can easily access information regarding all of his/her PVL licenses.  The portal account also provides self service features like resetting password and strong password requirement, ensuring secure access to licensee information.  Also reducing the need to call PVL staff for the license information in order to complete renewal or access their CE information.   Licensees can also  access their own historical transaction information, copies of the renewal transactions are available within the renewal history.  It also provides any new or future PVL licensees to track application status as well.
